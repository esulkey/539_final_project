Fudge Brownies


Source: “King Arthur Gluten Free Ultimate Fudge Brownie Mix”

Yield: 1 pan of brownies

Ingredients

  • 1 package King Arthur Gluten Free Ultimate Fudge Brownie Mix
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup chips or nuts (optional)

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350F if using light metal pan (325F if using glass or dark metal pan). Grease an 8” square or 9” round pan.
  • Whisk together melted butter, water, and eggs. Stir in mix (and chips/nuts). Spread batter evenly in greased pan.
  • Bake for 35-40 minutes if using light or dark metal pan or 40-45 minutes if using glass pan. When brownies are done, toothpick inserted into center will show a few moist crumbs but no raw batter. Cool at least 15 minutes before cutting.
