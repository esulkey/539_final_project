Hummus


Source: “Jasmine’s Homemade Hummus” from The Disney Princess Cookbook

Yield: 2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can garbanzo beans
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Pita bread

Instructions

  • Drain the garbanzo beans and combine with the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, and salt in the bowl of a food processor or blender.
  • Blend ingredients until smooth.
  • Spoon the hummus into a small bowl. Serve with pita bread triangles.
