Banana Vanilla Smoothie


Source: “Sweet Sea-Foam Smoothie” from The Disney Princess Cookbook

Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 cup vanilla frozen yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions

  • Break the banana into pieces and put them into a blender.
  • Add the water, frozen yogurt, and lime juice.
  • Blend the ingredients until they are smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the smoothie into a tall glass, and serve with a straw.
