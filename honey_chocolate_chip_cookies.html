Honey Chocolate Chip Cookies


Source: “Kids Bake ‘Em Cookies” from Mrs. Fields Cookie Book

Yield: 2 dozen cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • ½ cup salted butter softened
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup honey
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 300F.
  • In a small bowl combine flour, soda and salt. Mix well with a wire whisk and set aside.
  • In another medium bowl blend butter, sugar and honey with an electric mixer at medium speed. Beat until light and soft, then scrape sides of bowl.
  • Add the flour mixture and chocolate chips, and blend at low speed just until combined. Do not overmix.
  • Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets, 1 ½ inches apart. Bake 18-20 minutes or until light golden brown. Immediately transfer cookies with a spatula to a cool, flat surface.
